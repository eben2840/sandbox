D-KEB Express Global Ltd.

Industries Served

D-KEB Express Global Ltd. is proud to serve a range of critical industries, providing tailored support services that help our clients achieve their operational goals:

• Oil & Gas: With expertise in upstream, midstream, and downstream operations, we partner with major oil and gas companies to deliver PPE, materials, and logistical support that ensure safe and efficient operations.
• Energy Sector: We offer specialized solutions to support the production and distribution of energy, including the supply of essential materials and technical support.
• Mining: Our services extend to the mining sector, where we provide the necessary equipment and supplies to support safety and productivity in challenging environments.

Quality & Safety Standards

• Quality Assurance: Our quality management system is designed to ensure that every product and service we deliver meets or exceeds client expectations. We work closely with our OEM partners to provide certified equipment and materials that adhere to international standards like ISO 9001.
• Safety Compliance: Safety is a top priority in all our operations. Our PPE products comply with stringent safety certifications, including ISO 3873, ISO 20345, and EN standards. We also provide training and guidance to ensure safe practices on-site.
• Certifications: We are certified by the Energy Commission and licensed by the Petroleum Commission, ensuring our compliance with industry regulations and local content requirements. Our dedication to meeting the highest standards ensures that we remain a trusted partner in the industry.

Projects and Experience
